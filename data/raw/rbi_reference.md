# RBI UPI Reference Data

Source: Reserve Bank of India — DBIE Portal
URL: https://dbie.rbi.org.in

## Key Statistics Used for Simulation Calibration

- UPI monthly transactions (2023): ~8–10 billion per month nationally
- Average transaction value: ₹1,500–2,000
- Overall fraud rate: ~0.002% of volume (value-wise higher)
- Top states by UPI volume: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Delhi
- Success rate industry average: ~91–93%

## How simulation mirrors RBI data
- State distribution weighted by actual UPI adoption rates
- Amount ranges calibrated per merchant category
- Fraud rates set higher than real (2–5%) for analytical demonstration
- Festival dates match actual Indian calendar 2023–2024
